# Immersion & Features

- **Jammer / signal degradation** — for danger zones, a jammer effect that
  degrades visual and control fidelity: simulated "packet loss" (input/video
  dropouts) and a "snowy feed" visual on the FPV camera. Distinct from crash
  / SIGNAL LOST (P2 Phase C) — this would be a gradual, zone-based
  degradation rather than a hard cutoff.
- **Target/goal marking on the map** — mark objectives or waypoints on the
  HUD/map. Likely a prerequisite or companion to
  [quests-and-scenarios.md](quests-and-scenarios.md).
- **UI Menu** — DPad-controlled 2D menu to toggle various simulation flags,
  similar to Forza Horizon / F1 Series menus.
- **Payload physics** — if quests include "drop payload," carrying it should
  affect mass/CoG/handling, not just be a flag. Toggling/dropping is a
  natural fit for the DPad menu above.
- **Multi-camera / gimbal** — a gimbal-stabilized camera feed separate from
  the airframe-locked FPV. Switching feeds is another DPad-menu candidate.
- **Sound simulation** — floated, but Tim is skeptical it's more than a
  gimmick. Low priority.
